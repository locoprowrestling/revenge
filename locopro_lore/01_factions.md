# LoCo Pro Wrestling Lore – Factions

---

## 1. The Rising

[IN-UNIVERSE] Alignment: Babyface / resistance faction.

[IN-UNIVERSE] Ethos:

- Break old power structures.
- Give Longmont something honest to cheer for.
- Put power back into the hands of wrestlers and the crowd.

[IN-UNIVERSE] Key members and allies:

- **Zeak Gallent – “The Picture of Talent”** – Standard bearer and central hero.
- **Major Glory** – Patriotic, disciplined hero who insists on winning the right way.
- **Johnny Crash** – Punk-rock brawler, reckless but beloved.
- **Erza Menagerie Tinker** – Creative and chaotic wild card.
- **Carter Cash** – A high flying newcomer eager to prove his worth.
- The Longmont crowd is framed as part of The Rising’s movement.

---

## 2. The Pillars

[IN-UNIVERSE] Alignment: Heel establishment faction.

[IN-UNIVERSE] Ethos:

- “We built this place.”
- Protect the status quo and their own prestige.
- Use contracts, lawyers, and backstage power as weapons.

### 2.1 Power Structure

- **JT Staten** – Money man and true power-broker. Prefers to pull strings from the bar or office rather than wrestle.
- **Cody Devine – “Cloud King”** – Self-proclaimed leader and public face of The Pillars, but ultimately dependent on Staten’s money and influence.

### 2.2 Core Members and Associates

- **Cody Devine** – Schemer who weaponizes contracts and leverage, often using personal relationships as pawns.
- **JT Staten** – Executive-level villain overseeing the faction.
- **“Pretty Boy” Franky Gonzales** – Second-generation Pillars member under pressure from a powerful Pillars-aligned father.
- **“The Stupendous” Nicky Hyde** – Theatrical, masked monster; the faction’s chaos weapon.
- **“All Business” Dean Mercer** – Hired fist; a professional enforcer who sells his violence to the highest bidder.


[IN-UNIVERSE] The Pillars’ extended network includes unnamed managers, lawyers, and hangers-on who help enforce their control.
