## 1. Promotion Overview (Real World)

[OUT-OF-UNIVERSE] LoCo Pro Wrestling is an independent wrestling promotion based in Longmont, Colorado. It emphasizes cinematic, story-driven wrestling with strong local and community roots.

[OUT-OF-UNIVERSE] LoCo Pro Wrestling’s “Battle at the Opera House 2025” event marks its return to Longmont after roughly eight years, transforming the historic Dickens Opera House into a battleground for a long-form faction war between **The Rising** and **The Pillars**.

[OUT-OF-UNIVERSE] Battle at the Opera House 2025 takes place at the Dickens Opera House in downtown Longmont, Colorado, on Sunday, November 16, 2025 (doors 5 pm, bell 6 pm), framed as “LoCo Pro Wrestling returns to Longmont for the first time in 8 years.”

## 2. World Premise (In-Universe)

[IN-UNIVERSE] LoCo Pro Wrestling takes place in a heightened, mythologized version of Longmont, Colorado, often referred to as the **City of Lights**.

[IN-UNIVERSE] The **Dickens Opera House** is treated as sacred ground and the central battleground.

[IN-UNIVERSE] The core conflict is between:

- **The Rising** – a movement of wrestlers fighting for justice, change, and open doors.
- **The Pillars** – entrenched powers of control who claim they “hold the building up,” guarding the spotlight for themselves.

[IN-UNIVERSE] The **Battle at the Opera House – City of Lights Tournament** is the opening chapter of a long-term war between these factions.
