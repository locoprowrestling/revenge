# LoCo Pro Wrestling Lore – Locale and Flavor

---

## 1. City of Lights

[IN-UNIVERSE] Longmont, Colorado is presented as the **City of Lights**:
- Nighttime Main Street imagery.
- The glow of the Dickens Opera House marquee.
- A small town with outsize stories happening in its shadows and alleys.

[IN-UNIVERSE] The crowd is not just an audience; they are part of The Rising, positioned as Longmont’s heart pushing back against The Pillars.

---

## 2. Key Landmarks

[IN-UNIVERSE] **Dickens Opera House**
- Central battleground and symbolic heart of the promotion.
- The site of the City of Lights Tournament and the Battle at the Opera House.

[IN-UNIVERSE] Other recurring visual and narrative landmarks:
- **Main Street** – Rooftops, alleys, and street-level scenes can be used for vignettes and promos.
- **Old Sugar Mill** – Industrial, haunted-feeling backdrop suitable for grudge segments and violent confrontations.
- **St. Vrain River / Greenway** – Transitional, liminal space suitable for reflective promos or “meet at the bridge” type scenes.

[IN-UNIVERSE] Overall tone:
- Wrestlers are treated as modern folk heroes and villains of Longmont.
- Real places are mythologized into wrestling legend through promos, matches, and cinematic segments.
