# Match 4: Carter Cash vs. "The Stupendous One" Nicky Hyde

**Rising Rules:** Submissions Only

---

A score left unsettled at Battle at the Opera House comes back around at Vendetta at the Opera House as Carter Cash steps into a dangerous rematch with "The Stupendous One" Nicky Hyde.

Under Rising Rules: Submissions Only, there will be no shortcuts to victory. One of these men will have to make the other quit.

## Stakes: The Final Word

- **If Carter Cash wins:** Nicky Hyde must leave LoCo Pro Wrestling immediately and never return.
- **If Nicky Hyde wins:** He earns The Final Word — the right to address the Opera House crowd uninterrupted just before the main event begins.

---

For Carter, this is a chance at revenge and a chance to remove one of The Pillars' most dangerous weapons for good. For Hyde, this is not just a match. It is a demonstration. The masked monster who defeated Carter in the Battle at the Opera House tournament now looks to prove that the first time was no fluke.

Expect a vicious technical battle, painful submissions, and a fight where every mistake could decide the fate of the night.

## The Story

Hyde treats this as a demolition. There is some strong back-and-forth, but the skilled and devious Hyde comes out on top.

> **Result:** Hyde wins and earns The Final Word.
