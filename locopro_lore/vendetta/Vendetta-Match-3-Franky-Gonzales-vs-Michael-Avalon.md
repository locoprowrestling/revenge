# Match 3: Michael Avalon vs. Franky Gonzales

**Rising Rules:** No Rope Breaks

---

They came into Vendetta at the Opera House as Pretty Great, a proven championship tag team with gold to their name — but on this night, Michael Avalon and Franky Gonzales are set to stand on opposite sides of the fight.

Together, Avalon and Franky are the **Primos Premier Pro Wrestling Tag Team Champions** and hold 2/3 of the **CSW/CSC Trios Championships**. But titles and history won't mean anything when the bell rings at the Dickens Opera House.

## Stakes: JT Staten's Substitution Clause

- **If Avalon wins:** JT Staten will be unable to substitute anyone else and will be legally forced to compete in the main event himself.
- **If Franky Gonzales wins:** Staten stays protected at the bar and names a proxy to fight in his place.

---

This is more than a match between partners. It is a collision of loyalty, power, and control — with the main event hanging in the balance.
