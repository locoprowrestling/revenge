# Match 2: Johnny Crash vs. "All Business" Dean Mercer

**Rising Rules:** Falls Count Anywhere Street Fight

---

At Vendetta at the Opera House, the fight for control will get uglier fast.

Under Rising Rules, this match will be a Falls Count Anywhere Street Fight as Johnny Crash collides with "All Business" Dean Mercer in a battle that could shape the rest of the night.

## Stakes: The Law

- **If Johnny Crash wins:** The Rising will select the referee for the City of Lights Elimination Gauntlet.
- **If Dean Mercer wins:** The Pillars will choose the referee and tighten their grip on the law before the biggest match of the night.

---

This is not just about two men settling a score. It is about who controls the rules when everything is on the line.

And if that were not enough, the fallout promises to be chaos. With tensions boiling over and both sides circling the ring, this street fight could spark a full-scale war between The Rising and The Pillars.

## Post-Match

Both Codah and Hussy rush the ring and start a beatdown on Crash as Mercer heads to the back. That only lasts until a man in leather pants rushes in to help Johnny Crash.

That man is **Niles Blood**.

Hussy bails, and Niles delivers a jarring kick to Codah Alexander, taking him out. Niles helps Johnny to his feet, and Johnny brushes himself off. Other members of The Rising then rush in after Niles makes the save. Niles returns to his seat in the crowd.

> **Result:** Mercer wins. The City of Lights Elimination Gauntlet will have a corrupt official.
