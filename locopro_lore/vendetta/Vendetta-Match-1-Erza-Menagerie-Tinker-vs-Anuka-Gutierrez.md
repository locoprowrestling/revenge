# Match 1: ERZA Menagerie Tinker vs. Anuka Gutierrez

**Rising Rules:** The Ol' Switcharoo

---

What was originally announced as ERZA vs. Anuka Gutierrez is set to become something very different at Vendetta at the Opera House.

JT Staten claims his substitution clause is still in effect, and rather than let Anuka simply win his freedom, he plans to substitute his #1 enforcer into the match. But ERZA sees the move coming.

Under Rising Rules: The Ol' Switcharoo, ERZA will have the right to make a substitution of her own — and intends to put Anuka Gutierrez into the fight to battle for his own freedom.

## Stakes: Anuka's Freedom

- **If Anuka wins:** He will earn his freedom and be legally cleared to compete in the main event.
- **If Hussy Steele wins:** Anuka will be barred from the building for the rest of the night.

---

This will be more than a match. It will be a fight for Anuka's future, his freedom, and his chance to stand with The Rising.

> **Result:** Substitution leads to **Hussy Steele vs. Anuka Gutierrez**
