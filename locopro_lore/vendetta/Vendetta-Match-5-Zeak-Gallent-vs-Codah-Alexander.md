# Match 5: Zeak Gallent vs. Codah Alexander

**Rising Rules:** No stipulations. One-on-one match. One fall. May the best man win.

---

At Vendetta at the Opera House, Zeak Gallent and Codah Alexander went one on one with no stipulations, no shortcuts, and no excuses. Control over the opening pair in the City of Lights Elimination Gauntlet was on the line.

## Stakes: The Opening Pair

- **If Zeak Gallent wins:** The Rising would choose which member of The Pillars had to start the Gauntlet.
- **If Codah Alexander wins:** The Pillars would choose which member of The Rising had to start the match.

---

This was a straight-up fight between two of the night's most important players, and the result helped shape everything that followed.

> **Result:** Zeak wins. He sets the trap — forcing **JT Staten vs. Zeak Gallent** to open the City of Lights Elimination Gauntlet.
