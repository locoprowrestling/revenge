## Match Rules

For this final match, there can only be **4 competitors in the ring at a time**.

- The first 2 competitors start.
- 2 minutes later, a 3rd person joins.
- 2 minutes later, a 4th person joins.
- Once the 4th competitor joins, new competitors are sent out as eliminations occur, regardless of time.

## Phase 3: The Purge and the Connection

**Elimination 7 — The Punishment:** Staten, Hyde, and Codah eliminate Franky Gonzales *(Over the Top Rope)* — to punish his earlier failure.

**24:00 — Entrant 11:** Johnny Crash *(Rising)*

**Elimination 8:** Johnny Crash eliminates Codah Alexander *(Over the Top Rope)*

**25:30 — Entrant 12:** Niles Blood *(Rebel/Rising)*

**Elimination 9:** Crash and Blood eliminate Nicky Hyde *(Double-Team, Pinfall)*

**30:00 — Entrant 13:** Hussy Steele *(Pillars)*

**Elimination 10 — The Electricity:**

The ring splits:
- Blood vs. Staten
- Crash vs. Hussy

Staten tries to attack Niles from behind, but Blood beats Staten into a corner. Hussy beats Crash into the opposite corner. Blood and Hussy both stop. They spin around, fists ready to strike each other — but they lock eyes. The world around them disappears. The electricity is real.

Staten seizes the opening, smashes Blood from behind, and dumps him over the top rope.

> **Niles Blood is eliminated.**

**Elimination 11:** Hussy is stunned, looking over the ropes at the fallen Niles. Johnny Crash recovers and immediately dumps the distracted Hussy over the top rope.

> **Hussy Steele is eliminated.**

It comes down to **Johnny Crash vs. JT Staten**.

1. Staten uses brass knuckles to level Crash while the corrupt ref is "distracted."
2. Staten gloats, certain of victory.
3. As Crash begins to stir, showing his signature grit — Carter Cash's music hits.

**30:00 — Entrant 14:** Carter Cash *(???)*

1. Carter enters with a steel chair, seemingly the "cavalry."

3. He drops the chair, steps over the ropes to self-eliminate, and walks away in silence.

**Elimination 12:** Carter Cash *(Self-Elimination)*

**The Finish:** Staten pins the unconscious Crash.

**Elimination 13:** Johnny Crash
