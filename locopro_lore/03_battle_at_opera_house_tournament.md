# LoCo Pro Wrestling Lore – Battle at the Opera House: City of Lights Tournament

---

## 1. Event Frame

[IN-UNIVERSE] Name: **Battle at the Opera House – City of Lights Tournament**

[IN-UNIVERSE] Venue: Dickens Opera House, Longmont (“City of Lights”).

[IN-UNIVERSE] Stakes:
- Crown the first **City of Lights Tournament winner**.
- Establish whether The Rising or The Pillars hold early control over LoCo Pro Wrestling’s future.

---

## 2. Official First-Round Bracket (Canon)

### Match 1 – Erza Menagerie Tinker vs Cody Devine (Substitution Twist)

- Booked: **Erza** vs **Cody Devine**.
- Before the match, Cody produces a contract signed by Commissioner Adam Starling allowing him to substitute any wrestler into his first-round match.
- Cody forces **Anuka Gutierrez** (Erza’s boyfriend) to wrestle in his place.
- The match becomes an emotional disaster for both Erza and Anuka, orchestrated by Cody from ringside.
- **Advancement:** Cody takes credit and advances in the tournament based on Anuka’s forced participation.

---

### Match 2 – Zeak Gallent (The Rising) vs “Pretty Boy” Franky Gonzales (The Pillars)

- Faction showcase: The Rising vs The Pillars.
- Zeak and Franky wrestle a competitive match that underlines both men’s talent and ideology.
- **Result:** Zeak defeats Franky clean.
- Post-match: Zeak offers Franky a chance to walk away from The Pillars and join him on a better path.
- Franky refuses, clearly conflicted but still tied to The Pillars and his family obligations.
- **Advancement:** Zeak Gallent.

---

### Match 3 – Major Glory vs JT Staten (Substitution to Dean Mercer)

- Booked: **Major Glory** vs **JT Staten**.
- Staten presents his own substitution contract, signed by Commissioner Adam Starling.
- He sends **“All Business” Dean Mercer** as his proxy and heads to the bar instead of wrestling.
- Major Glory battles Dean Mercer in a hard-hitting match.
- **Result:** Major Glory defeats Dean Mercer.
- **Advancement:** Major Glory.

---

### Match 4 – Nicky Hyde vs Johnny Crash (Ambush and Replacement)

- Booked: **Nicky Hyde** vs **Johnny Crash**.
- Before the bell, Hyde ambushes Crash and injures him so badly he cannot compete.
- **Carter Cash** rushes in to protect Crash and volunteers to wrestle in his place.
- The official match becomes **Nicky Hyde vs Carter Cash**.
- **Result:** Hyde brutalizes Carter and wins, establishing himself as a monstrous threat.
- **Advancement:** Nicky Hyde.

---

## 3. Round Two (Semi-Finals)

Round Two tightened the Round One fallout: The Pillars’ power plays vs The Rising’s resolve.

### Semi-final 1 – Cody Devine (with Anuka Gutierrez) vs Major Glory

- Context: Cody already warped Round One by forcing Anuka into his match with Erza; Glory fought through Dean Mercer after Staten’s contract substitution. Both stories collided here.
- Match tone: Control vs momentum. Cody tried to slow the pace and weaponize frustration; Glory tried to keep it a straight fight.
- Finish: **Cody Devine advances**, aided by Anuka’s presence and a referee payoff that tipped the balance.

### Semi-final 2 – Nicky Hyde vs Zeak Gallent

- Context: Nicky reached Round Two by ambushing Johnny Crash and forcing Carter Cash into a replacement bout. Zeak advanced cleanly over Franky Gonzales.
- Match tone: Zeak pushed for pace and a fair contest; Nicky kept hunting shortcuts and rhythm breaks.
- Finish: **Zeak Gallent advances** via count-out when Nicky’s hubris backfires. Afterward Nicky tried to assault the referee, only to be laid out by Zeak to close the first half of the tournament.

---

## 4. Final and Tournament Outcome
### Double-elimination stipulation

- The gateway to the finals runs as a double-elimination; competitors who do not advance to the final via the first two rounds receive a second shot in the **CITY OF LIGHTS BATTLE ROYALE**.

#### CITY OF LIGHTS BATTLE ROYALE – elimination order

- Anuka Gutierrez eliminated Carter Cash.
- Nicky Hyde eliminated Johnny Crash.
- ERZA eliminated Franky Gonzales.
- Major Glory eliminated Anuka Gutierrez.
- Nicky Hyde eliminated ERZA.
- Major Glory eliminated Nicky Hyde.
- **“All Business” Dean Mercer eliminated Major Glory** to win the City of Lights Battle Royale and move on to the tournament finals.

### Tournament Finals (Triple Threat)

- Finalists: **Dean Mercer** (Battle Royale winner), **Cody Devine** (tournament bracket), **Zeak Gallent** (tournament bracket).
- Stakes: Two Pillars versus The Rising’s anchor, with contracts, muscle, and momentum colliding in one match.
- Result: **Zeak Gallent overcomes the odds to win the City of Lights Tournament**, standing tall as The Rising’s statement win over The Pillars.
